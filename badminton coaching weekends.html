BADMINTON COACHING WEEKENDS

Tres Badminton Academy is supervised personally by Sheen Kim. The Academy is home for badminton players in various levels (advance, intermediate, beginners), and on different age brackets. The Academy mainly focuses on coaching young individuals aged 6-12, providing step-step training with the basics.

Sheen Kim is a profecient coach, can train different levels (beginners, intermediate, and advance). Her first coaching experience was in Ipil, Zamboanga Sibugay, when she was invited by the Mayor of the town to be one of the coaches for badminton in their first Ipil Sports Fest Clinic. She handled a total of 40 boys aged 8-12 beginners.

Coach Sheen offers weekends coaching for all levels, and in any age-bracket. She also has a Summer Badminton Clinic every year during summer breaks. If you are interested for a proper coaching, please leave us a message!
